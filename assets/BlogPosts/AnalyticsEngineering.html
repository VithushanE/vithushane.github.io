What is analytics Engineering?

```

Analytics Engineering is the process of building data pipelines and models that transform raw data into data products that can be used to provide insights

Some core areas of analytics engineering include:

  • Data Pipelines – moving data from source systems into databases and warehouses
  • ETL/ELT Processes – extracting, transforming, and loading data efficiently
  • Data Warehousing – organizing data for reporting and analytics
  • Cloud Platforms – leveraging services such as AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud

Data engineers help ensure that data is accurate, reliable, and accessible so analysts, data scientists, and business teams can generate insights and make informed decisions.
```
